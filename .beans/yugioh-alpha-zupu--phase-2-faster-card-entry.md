Roadmap Phase 2 (docs/Roadmap.md): make it faster and easier to add many cards to the inventory. Product outcome: users can add cards quickly without relying only on manual search and entry.

Scope from the roadmap, broken down into child feature beans:

- review and correction flow with suggested matches before cards are saved (shared entry pipeline)
- bulk entry workflows
- photo-based card recognition
- OCR-assisted entry
- voice input

Depends on Phase 1 (yugioh-alpha-brmu), in particular the inventory entry model (yugioh-alpha-wxy4).
